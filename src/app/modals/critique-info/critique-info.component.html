Critiquing a photo

Critiquing a photo allows you to give genuine feedback on a photo. There are 10 criteria for which you can give a score out of 100, as well as a section to comment on each criteria. The total score for a photo is know as the Collective Score.

You can invite a member of Shoot The Frame, or anyone in the world to critique your photos. If you are logged in, you can go to the Critiques section to see all of the Given and Received critiques.
