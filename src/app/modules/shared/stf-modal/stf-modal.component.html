STF Awards

The STF Awards were created in 2012. While the Shoot The Frame community has grown and morphed a little, the STF Awards are still at the centre of our mission.

Every month there are 3 awards, the categories remain the same. Shoot The Face (portrait), Shoot The Land (landscape) and Shoot The Wild (wildlife).

Entries close on the last day of every month.
